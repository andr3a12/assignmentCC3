COSTS


IF YOU APPLY FOR CAMPCANADA YOU WILL RECEIVE:


  • Work permit assistance & advice – you’ll receive the required documents and directions to apply for your work permit upon entry in Canada.
  • Confirmed Placement at a Canadian Summer Camp.
  • Inclusive Health Insurance.
  • Accommodation & Food for duration of time at camp.
  • Competitive salary based on age – more info below.
  • Fun and informative UK based orientation.
  • Flight assistance option – more info below.
  • Canadian bank account & social security card.
  • 24 hour emergency support/line from experienced staff.
  • The opportunity to meet tons of other international staff going to work at summer camps in Canada!
